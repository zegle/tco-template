Company Info

Turbo Charged Outfitters specializes in unique aerodynamic parts for multiple makes and brands of vehicles. What makes our company special is we offer lightweight aluminum composite material that is much more durable and sturdy than the typical fiberglass material most other companies use. With our product you do not need to worry about scratching your aero easily as our product is powder coated in a 3 stage process making the surface very durable to weather and road elements. You also do not need to worry about scrapping or rocks destroying the splitter like you would with the similar fiberglass aero. Our aluminum technology makes it so your aero parts can take a serious beating.
